How TSI Ratings Create Irrefutable Trust

Tokenized Integrity

We convert expert-audited scores into verifiable digital assets (tokens), locking the data permanently onto the blockchain. This eliminates data tampering and fraud.

Augmented Collateral

The TSI Ratings token augments traditional collateral, giving lenders the operational assurance and verifiable proof required to confidently underwrite high-ticket MSME loans.

Operational Predictor

Our score is a leading indicator of resilience. We measure an MSME's adoption of modern systems, directly reflecting their operational effectiveness and innovation.

The Dual Assessment Framework

DMA: Digital Maturity Assessment (MSMEs)

For Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs).

The DMA is an IT Auditor-led audit of the MSME's internal systems, scoring their adoption of cloud, digital accounting, cybersecurity, and API integration. This score provides trust in the borrower's operational quality.

CMA: Capability Maturity Assessment (Tech Providers)

For Technology Providers and Vendors serving MSMEs.

The CMA verifies the quality, security, and scalability of technology services (e.g., cloud platforms, specialized software) used by MSMEs. This score provides trust in the underlying technology ecosystem.
